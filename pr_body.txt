## Key Findings

### Root Cause Identified
The kernel is compiled as an ELF file, but GRUB 2 expects a raw binary with the multiboot header at offset 0. The ELF header comes before the multiboot header in the file, causing GRUB 2 to report "no multiboot header found".

### Recommended Solution
Convert ELF to raw binary using `objcopy`, which will place the multiboot header at the correct position (offset 0).
