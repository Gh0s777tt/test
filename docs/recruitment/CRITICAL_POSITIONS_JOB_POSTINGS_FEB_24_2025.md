## Position 1: Formal Verification Lead

### Job Title
**Formal Verification Lead**  
**Location**: Remote / San Francisco Bay Area  
**Type**: Full-time  
**Salary**: $180,000/year + $8,000 signing bonus  
**Reports To**: CTO

### About VantisOS
VantisOS is a formally verified microkernel operating system written in Rust, designed for maximum security, performance, and reliability. We're building the next generation of operating systems with formal verification at its core, ensuring mathematical proofs of correctness for critical components.

### Role Overview
We're seeking an experienced Formal Verification Lead to drive our formal verification efforts across the entire VantisOS codebase. You will lead a team of verification engineers, establish verification methodologies, and ensure that critical system components meet the highest standards of correctness and security.

### Key Responsibilities
- Lead the formal verification strategy and roadmap for VantisOS
- Design and implement formal verification proofs using Verus, Kani, and other tools
- Mentor and manage a team of formal verification engineers
- Establish best practices and methodologies for formal verification
- Collaborate with kernel developers to design verifiable systems
- Review and approve verification proofs and specifications
- Present verification results to stakeholders and auditors
- Stay current with the latest research in formal verification

### Required Qualifications
- PhD or Master's degree in Computer Science, Mathematics, or related field
- 5+ years of experience in formal verification
- Expert knowledge of Verus, Kani, or similar verification tools
- Strong background in theorem proving and type theory
- Experience with Rust programming language
- Proven track record of leading verification teams
- Excellent communication and leadership skills

### Preferred Qualifications
- Publications in formal verification or programming languages
- Experience with operating systems or microkernels
- Knowledge of security certifications (EAL 7+, FIPS 140-3)
- Experience with CI/CD for verification pipelines
- Familiarity with Coq, Isabelle, or Lean

### What We Offer
- Competitive salary ($180,000/year) + $8,000 signing bonus
- Remote-first work environment
- Equity in a groundbreaking startup
- Opportunity to work on cutting-edge technology
- Professional development budget
- Health, dental, and vision insurance
- Unlimited PTO

### How to Apply
