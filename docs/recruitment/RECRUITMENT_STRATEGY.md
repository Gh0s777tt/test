## Interview Process

### Screening Phase
1. **Resume Review** - 15-30 minutes
2. **Cover Letter Review** - 10-15 minutes
3. **Initial Screening Call** - 30 minutes

### Technical Assessment
1. **Coding Challenge** - 2-4 hours (take-home)
2. **System Design** - 1-2 hours (take-home)
3. **Technical Interview** - 60-90 minutes (video)

### Cultural Fit
1. **Behavioral Interview** - 45-60 minutes
2. **Team Interview** - 45-60 minutes
3. **Culture Fit Assessment** - 30 minutes

### Final Decision
1. **Reference Checks** - 2-3 references
2. **Background Check** - Standard background check
3. **Offer Preparation** - Salary, equity, benefits

## Risk Mitigation

### Recruitment Risks
1. **Talent Shortage** - High demand for Rust and systems developers
   - **Mitigation**: Expand search globally, offer competitive compensation
2. **Long Hiring Process** - Technical roles require extensive evaluation
   - **Mitigation**: Streamline process, parallel interviews
3. **Offer Rejection** - Candidates may accept other offers
   - **Mitigation**: Competitive packages, quick decision-making

### Budget Risks
1. **Funding Delays** - VC funding may take longer than expected
   - **Mitigation**: Multiple funding sources, phased hiring
2. **Cost Overruns** - Salaries may be higher than expected
   - **Mitigation**: Salary ranges, equity compensation

### Onboarding Risks
1. **Slow Ramp-Up** - New hires may take time to become productive
   - **Mitigation**: Comprehensive onboarding, mentorship
2. **Cultural Mismatch** - New hires may not fit team culture
   - **Mitigation**: Cultural fit interviews, trial period
