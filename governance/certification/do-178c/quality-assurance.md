# Software Quality Assurance Plan (SQAP)

---

## 1. QA Objectives

- Ensure compliance with DO-178C
- Ensure process adherence
- Ensure documentation completeness

---

## 2. Audits

- Process audits
- Product audits
- Configuration audits

---

## 3. Non-Conformance

All non-conformances shall be:
- Documented
- Tracked
- Resolved prior to release
