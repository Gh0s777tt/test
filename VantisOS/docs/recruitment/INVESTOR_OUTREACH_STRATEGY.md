## Meeting Preparation

### Pre-Meeting Checklist

**Research**:
- [ ] Research investor's portfolio companies
- [ ] Research investor's investment thesis
- [ ] Research investor's recent investments
- [ ] Identify potential synergies
- [ ] Prepare talking points

**Materials**:
- [ ] Print pitch deck (2 copies)
- [ ] Print funding proposal (2 copies)
- [ ] Print executive summary (2 copies)
- [ ] Prepare demo (laptop, projector)
- [ ] Prepare technical documentation (digital)
- [ ] Prepare business cards

**Practice**:
- [ ] Practice pitch presentation
- [ ] Anticipate questions
- [ ] Prepare answers to common questions
- [ ] Time presentation (aim for 20 minutes)

### During Meeting

**Presentation**:
- Start with problem statement
- Show demo of VantisOS
- Highlight competitive advantages
- Emphasize market opportunity
- Present investment terms
- Leave time for Q&A

**Key Talking Points**:
- "We're the first to market with production-ready formally verified OS"
- "7+ certifications vs competitors with 1-2"
- "$240B total addressable market"
- "10x ROI within 5 years"
- "Team scaling to 15 members with funding"

**Questions to Anticipate**:
1. Why formal verification?
2. How do you compete with Linux/Windows?
3. What's your go-to-market strategy?
4. Who are your target customers?
5. What's your timeline to profitability?
6. What's your exit strategy?
7. Why now?
8. What's the biggest risk?

- **Risk**: No term sheets

- **Risk**: Funding takes longer than expected
